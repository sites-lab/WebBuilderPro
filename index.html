Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Basic websites: 1-2 working days
Standard websites: 3-4 working days
Premium websites: 5-7 working days

Do I need to purchase hosting separately?

Yes, hosting is a separate service. We offer reliable hosting starting at €4/month with 2GB storage, unlimited bandwidth, and free SSL certificate.

Can I update my website content myself?

No, website content can only be updated by our team. However, we offer maintenance plans where we handle all content updates for you. This ensures your website remains secure and professional.

What happens after the website is launched?

We provide 30 days of support for any technical issues. After that, we recommend choosing a maintenance plan to keep your website secure and updated.

Do you provide SEO services?

Yes, all our packages include basic SEO optimization.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers and PayPal. A 20% deposit is required to start the project, with the balance due upon completion.

Can I see examples of your work?

Yes! We have example websites for each package type. Check out the example section above each package to see what kind of website you'll get.
